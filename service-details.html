Technical SEO

Technical SEO focuses on strengthening the foundation of your website to improve its overall performance, making it faster, easier to crawl and index by search engines, and providing a better experience for your visitors. Unlike content-based SEO, technical SEO deals with backend improvements that are crucial for search engines to understand and rank your site effectively.

Why is Technical SEO important?
A well-optimized technical setup ensures that your website loads quickly, works perfectly on all devices, and is easily discoverable by search engines. This leads to better rankings, higher organic traffic, and improved user satisfaction.

Here’s what our Technical SEO service includes:

  • Site Speed Optimization (90+ Core Web Vitals)
    We optimize your website’s loading speed to meet Google’s Core Web Vitals standards, ensuring fast page loads, smooth interactions, and visual stability. This helps reduce bounce rates and keeps visitors engaged.
  • Mobile Usability Enhancement
    With the majority of users Browse on mobile devices, we ensure your site is fully responsive and easy to navigate on smartphones and tablets. Mobile usability is a key ranking factor and essential for reaching your audience wherever they are.
  • Structured Data & Schema Integration
    We add structured data markup to your pages to help search engines understand your content better. This can improve how your listings appear in search results with rich snippets like ratings, pricing, and event details, boosting your click-through rates.
  • Crawlability & Indexability Audits
    We perform thorough audits to identify and fix any issues that might prevent search engines from crawling or indexing your site properly. This includes fixing broken links, optimizing robots.txt and sitemap files, and resolving duplicate content issues.

Our goal is to build a technically sound website that not only meets search engine requirements but also delivers a seamless and enjoyable experience to your visitors, ultimately driving more traffic and conversions.
